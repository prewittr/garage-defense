## User Story
**As a** Trapped Mechanic (player)
**I want** to drag scrap metal onto damage points on a failing garage door while the door is under rhythmic assault
**So that** I feel the tension-relief cycle that is the entire pitch of the game

## Acceptance Criteria

### Core mechanic
- [ ] **Given** a door at 100% HP with 10 scrap units in inventory, **when** I drag a scrap unit to a damage point on the door, **then** door HP restores by **15%** at the exact drop coordinate and the scrap count decreases by 1.
- [ ] **Given** a scrap drag in progress, **when** I release outside any damage point, **then** the scrap snaps back to inventory and no HP change occurs.
- [ ] **Given** the door has no holes (HP == 100%), **when** I drag a scrap unit onto it, **then** the action is rejected and the scrap returns (no wasted resource).

### Impact loop
- [ ] **Given** the scene is running, **when** a `ZombieHit` event fires (every **1.5s**), **then** door HP drops by **5%** and screen-shake + dust-fall VFX trigger.
- [ ] **Given** current HP, **when** impact fires, **then** screen-shake magnitude = `(1 − HP/MaxHP)` applied through a critically-damped spring.
- [ ] **Given** the player accessibility flag `ReduceMotion = true`, **when** impact fires, **then** camera shake is skipped (dust-fall still plays).

### First-pass juice (Malik's slice)
- [ ] **Given** a successful scrap placement, **when** weld completes, **then** a **spark VFX** plays at the drop coord and `SFX_Scrap_Weld` (clink-hiss) fires.
- [ ] **Given** a zombie impact, **when** it lands, **then** `SFX_Metal_Impact` plays with pitch randomized 0.9–1.1.
- [ ] All weld-spark draw calls are batched in a single SRP pass (no SRP batcher break).

### Perf gate (hard block)
- [ ] **Given** a drag gesture, **when** measured, **then** input-to-visual latency **p95 ≤ 12ms** on desktop profiler (validated via [RPM-008](RPM-008.md)).
- [ ] Zero GC alloc in the drag-update tick (verified via Profiler capture).
